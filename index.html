/punkygruv — translation guide

Insight disruptivo: antisistema pero bailable; crónico, irónico y comunitario.

> INITIATE SYSTEM [ PUNKYGRUV ]
> STATUS: DISRUPTIVE / ONGOING / HERMETIC-OPEN

> load_techno --module hardgroove, acid, ebm, tekno
> inject_tropical --samples cumbia, salsa, vallenato, tropicanibalismo
> override_punk --ska-punk-latino, reggae-roots
> fuse --mode punkygruv

/press — Bio & micro
            
150-word bio

              
NEVO is a DJ/producer and sonic curator from Bogotá whose sets move between techno, EBM, hardcore and latinclub while tracing lines back to cumbia, salsa and regional ska-punk. Working under the literary alias Nebo, he publishes poems and prose that echo the same questions driving his mixes: who holds a genre’s memory, and how do communities shape the dance? Trained in business and anthropology, he approaches music as a living archive: research while creating, create while researching, document with care, credit sources, return value to the scenes that made us. NEVO inhabits genres instead of forcing them; each track is a testimony, each set a conversation. His practice is alternative, disruptive and empathetic, inviting listeners to cross bridges without erasing differences. Upcoming work includes hybrid performances that weave spoken text with club energy, and collaborative projects with local collectives, labs and cultural institutions.
